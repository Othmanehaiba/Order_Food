FAQ


What are the delivery times ?

Delivery times vary depending on the chosen method and the destination. For local deliveries, you can generally expect 24 to 48 business hours; for standard national deliveries, allow 3 to 5 business days. During peak periods (sales, holidays), these times may be slightly extended. Once your order is confirmed, you will receive an email/SMS with an estimated timeframe and a tracking number.

In-store pickup (click & collect) is simple ?

you place your order online and select ‘in-store pickup’ at checkout, then you will receive a confirmation when your order is ready. Present your confirmation email and an ID at the counter to collect your package. Uncollected orders are generally kept for 7 to 14 days before being cancelled, depending on the store’s policy

Can orders be placed in advance ?

Yes, it is possible to place orders in advance. When ordering, select the desired delivery or pickup date if this option is available. Please note that for certain products (pre-orders, out-of-stock items), the estimated date may be delayed and will be clearly indicated on the product page. Cancellation or modification conditions vary depending on the order status (pre-order vs. available stock).

What are the delivery fees? ?

Delivery fees depend on the weight/volume of the order, the chosen delivery method, and the delivery area. We often offer standard delivery (fixed rate or calculated at checkout), express delivery (higher rate), and sometimes free delivery for orders over a certain amount. The exact fee will be displayed before payment confirmation.

What payment methods are accepted ?

We accept cash on delivery in certain areas. For in-store pickups, payment on-site by card or cash may be possible depending on the store. The specific options are displayed at checkout.
